Abstract-From  the heartwood of Croton sonderianus was isolated a new furanoid diterpene, sonderianin.  The  structure was elucidated by spectral analysis, X-ray studies and chemical evidence.  INTRODUCTION  The benzene  extract  of the heartwood  of Croton  sondeiianus Muell. Arg. has been shown to have specific  activity  against  Mycobacterium  smegmatis  and  Staphylococcus aureus (J. D. McChesney  and L. A.  Mitscher, unpublished  results). From this extract was  isolated a novel diterpene, sonderianin  (l), bearing the  backbone-rearranged  clerodane-type carbon skeleton.
